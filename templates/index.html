Feature Modules

Each module maps to a practical workflow that can be demonstrated inside the prototype or explained in the architecture section.

Digital Arrest Alerting

Detects police, CBI, court, and arrest impersonation scripts and returns immediate citizen-safe guidance.

Counterfeit CV Agent

Accepts note images and simulates visual inspection of brightness, print clarity, and suspicious counterfeit indicators.

Citizen Fraud Shield

Turns suspicious SMS or chat messages into a risk level, category, confidence score, and recommended next action.

Geo Intelligence

Aggregates city-level fraud signals for operational heatmaps and region-aware escalation.

Fraud Network Graph

Links repeated cities, reports, and infrastructure into graph relationships for investigative prioritization.

Voice Scam Detection

Analyzes uploaded call audio and flags suspicious scam-call indicators for triage workflows.
